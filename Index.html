WHAT IS COMPETITIVE PROGRAMMING ?

Competitive Programming is utilising your problem-skills and logical ability to come up with a programming solution in the least possible time. Since there are plenty of solutions to a coding problem, you need to understand the concept of Time and Space Complexity meaning you need to find an optimal solution based on how much time and space an algorithm takes to solve a problem. Competitive programming is a mind sport usually held over the Internet or a local network, involving participants trying to program according to provided specifications. Competitive programming is recognized and supported by several multinational software and Internet companies, such as Google, Facebook and IBM.

There are several organizations who host programming competitions on a regular basis, some are CodeChef, Sphere Online Judge (SPOJ), TopCoder, etc..

I would really recommend you to try doing atleast 1-2 question, might not take much of your time, but if you liked it, even whole life would be less to completely explore it.

How to get started with COMPETITIVE PROGRAMMING ?

Competitive Programming - Choose a Programming Language your comfortable with and learn its syntax. - Study the fundamentals of Data Structures and Algorithms and understand their application. - Make use of the resources available on Codechef, InterviewBit, Topcoder, SPOJ & Participate in coding contests available on various coding platforms. - If your a beginner start from the basic level on these platforms and slowly advance to solving complex problems.
